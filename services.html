Our Services

Postertrack

Postertrack is our revolutionary web-based out of-home-media audit tool for tracking, evaluating and reporting all type of out-of-home media displays. Accessible to advertisers, agencies and media owners on license, this online tool generates real-time proof of performance and media market intelligence for out-of-home media planning, management and inventory control. With this tool, advertisers, agencies and media owners are able to access our online dashboard, track the status of their displays, get email alerts on displays with error as our field force upload images and data around the country and download reports in user-friendly formats.

Transtrack

Transtrack is our proof of performance audit service that generates compliance and competitive reports for transit and mobile out-of-home media formats including printed and electronic displays on and inside BRT buses, inter-state buses and coaches, taxis and trucks. Our Trans Auditors are positioned at major stops and stations designated routes, equipped with GPS enabled digital cameras to certify:

  • Aesthetic condition of the buses
  • Unhindered mobility of the buses along designated routes
  • Condition of campaign/display materials
  • Visibility of campaign to target audience

Cinetrack

Cinetrack is our proof of performance audit service dedicated to tracking advertising and promotional investments in and around cinemas and movie theatres. Our Cine Auditors get passes into and sit through screening to observe compliance to commercial screening schedules and placement of displays around the cinema space. Cinetrack also covers surveys and enquiries into the effectiveness and impact of ad placement in cinema tracking sound/audio quality and measuring audience response.

Outsight

Outsight is our dip stick survey conducted around selected out-of-home media issues, vehicles, formats, locations and campaigns aimed at gaining insight on what works or do not work on out-of-home. Our clients can buy reports of our surveys off the shelf or commission us to do custom surveys.

Geo-Map

Geo-Map is our outdoor planning tool powered by GPS technology which enables the determination of exact geo-spatial location of displays for viewing on map applications like Google Map and Google Earth. Critical for tracking market proximity, competitive proximity, compliance with local legislation and geographical media planning, Geo-Map is available for over 250 cities and towns in Nigeria.
