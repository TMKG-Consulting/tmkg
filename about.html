About Us

Who We Are

Established in 2005, TMKG is a knowledge and insight driven marketing and media management consultancy with multiple competencies in critical areas of business. Our audit practice pioneered the independent and structured tracking and evaluation of out-of-home media in Nigeria.

TMKG Consulting is a provider of independent third-party marketing, media performance measurement, research and market intelligence services to help brands improve their return on marketing programs and media investment. We specialize in aggregating, enriching and analyzing large volumes of data to unlock value for clients via insights for marketing and communication
